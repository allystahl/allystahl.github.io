About

Copenhagen Pastry differs from other bakeries through our emphasis on using authentic Danish recipes, traditional baking methods and the finest quality ingredients to create pastries that are flaky, light and delicately sweet without being sugary or heavy. Quality control, consistency and continuity are important to Copenhagen Pastry and among the reasons that our selections overall remain the same week after week. Our fillings are primarily almond paste and custard in various ratios along with cinnamon, almond flakes and traditional Danish fruit fillings (apple, raspberry, apricot and blueberry). We always have trays with samples available for new and returning customers at Copenhagen Pastry.
